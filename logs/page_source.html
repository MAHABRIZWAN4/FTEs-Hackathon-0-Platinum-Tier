    SCAM ALERT — For Remote INTERNSHIP OFFERS ON LINKEDIN 🚨 Hello everyone! 👋 There have been increasing reports of fake internship offers circulating on LinkedIn and other platforms, especially for summer internships. Please be careful and stay vigilant! ⚠️ Here are some common red flags of LinkedIn internship scams: 🔹 No interview or screening process — You’re “selected” immediately 🔹 Asks for payment — claiming registration fee, onboarding fee, or certificate fee 🔹 Communication via personal email or WhatsApp, not official company email 🔹 Unrealistic high stipends with no verification 🔹 Company has no real online presence or few/no employees listed. ⚠️ Important: Legitimate internships DO NOT ask for money to join or issue certificates. Always verify the company, check reviews, and double-check the offer. 📌 Tips to stay safe: ✔ Ask for an official interview ✔ Verify company profile and website ✔ Check if current employees are listed on LinkedIn ✔ Don’t share sensitive personal details or pay fees Stay smart and safe while advancing your career! 💼✨
